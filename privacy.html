SiteLedger Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2026

Summary: SiteLedger works offline, requires no account, includes no ads or analytics, and saves optional estimate drafts only on your device.

Data Collection

SiteLedger does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not include analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, social login, or account registration.

Local Storage

If you save an estimate draft, the draft details are stored locally in the app storage area on the same device.

Saved drafts remain on the device until you delete them in the app or clear the app data from Android settings.

Permissions

SiteLedger does not request Internet, location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage permissions.

The app may open your email app when you choose the support contact button. Sending email is handled by your email app, not by SiteLedger.

App Scope

SiteLedger is an offline equipment estimate planning tool. It does not submit booking requests, show live availability, process payments, or contact rental businesses automatically.
